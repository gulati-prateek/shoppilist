11. Things beyond just resolution to take care:
Aspect ratios: not just width/height but ratios like 16:9, 18:9, 19.5:9, 20:9 — notches and cutouts affect safe areas.
Pixel density (DPI/PPI): test @1x, @2x, @3x asset scaling; use dp/pt units, never hardcode px.
Safe areas / notches: iPhone notch/Dynamic Island, Android punch-holes, gesture nav bars.
Orientation: portrait and landscape, especially for tablets.
OS-level text scaling: test with large accessibility font sizes — layouts often break here before they break on screen size.
